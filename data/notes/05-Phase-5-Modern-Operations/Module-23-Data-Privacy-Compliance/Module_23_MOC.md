# Module 23: Data Privacy & Compliance

*Designing for regulation, sovereignty, and data protection.*

## Overview
How to build systems that respect user privacy, satisfy global regulations (GDPR, HIPAA), and manage the complex lifecycle of sensitive data.

## Topics
- [[Data Privacy and Compliance]] — PII classification, masking/tokenization, right to erasure, crypto-shredding, and consent management architecture.
- [[GDPR and Data Sovereignty]] — GDPR/CCPA/PIPL/LGPD frameworks, data residency patterns, cross-border transfer mechanisms, and right to erasure implementation.
- [[Data Classification and Access Control]] — Classification tiers, automated PII detection, RBAC/ABAC/ReBAC, row-level security, and dynamic masking.
- [[Audit Logging and Compliance]] — Audit event format, immutability/tamper-proofing, retention policies by regulation (SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOX, GDPR).
- [[Multi-Region E-Commerce]] — Scaling for global compliance.

## Learning Objectives
- Design for "Right to be Forgotten" with crypto-shredding.
- Understand data residency vs. data sovereignty.
- Implement PII vaults and tokenization layers.
- Architect audit trails for non-repudiation.
